About This Dashboard

The Soil Fertility Dashboard was created to help Rwandan farmers easily determine the right fertilizer blend and application rate for their fields. By leveraging historical soil datasets and a Random Forest model, this tool provides quick recommendations for popular crops like maize, beans, potatoes, and more.

Features:

  • Single‐sample prediction (soil & crop → fertilizer + kg/ha).
  • Batch predictions on hold‐out data to evaluate performance.
  • User profile with dark/light toggle & basic settings.
  • Agriculture‐related news and resources for Rwanda.
  • Fully responsive, modern UI built with Bootstrap 5 and Chart.js.

Data Source: Soil Nutrient Balance (NPK) Dataset of Rwanda, CC BY 4.0 (Wageningen University & Research, via Zenodo).

Developer: Uwase Clémentine, University of Rwanda, June 2025.
